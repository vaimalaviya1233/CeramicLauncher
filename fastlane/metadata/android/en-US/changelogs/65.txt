- Added Kustom variables support (go to Other->Kustom variables to enable)
- Improved settings a lot
- New settings:
   - Notification badges customizations
   - Notification swipe background color
   - A setting to ignore the navbar height
   - An option to disable the app drawer, in case you wanna use a custom one (like "smart drawer")
   - Option to keep scroll position when exiting the launcher
   - An option to have an undo popup after sliding a news card away
   - Notification action button corner radius
   - A setting to disable vertical fading edges in the feed
- Added a gesture trigger to open an app
- Fixed a bunch of bugs
